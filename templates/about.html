About Us..

We are observing a recent trend of increase in heart attack fatalities with every passing generation. With the consultations of medical experts, we have figured out, that with the increasing workload, stress, and unhealthy lifestyle, ther's an increase in the upwardness of the curve of heart attack victims. Due to the lack of awareness of the general masses, this trend is not going to reduce in the coming future. We, as a group of four, did a brainstorming session to figure out what we can do, and have tried our best to come to the solution.
